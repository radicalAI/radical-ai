led by Natalia Bilenko, Ria Kalluri, Rapha Gontijo Lopes, & Michelle Carney

Our Relationship with AI: Exploring the Present + Dreaming Up Radical Futures was a Radical AI Project workshop at the CtrlZ.AI zine fair in association with the FAccT 2020 conference in Barcelona, Spain. The workshop consisted of two parts:
Dreaming Up Radical AI Futures and A Day in the Life with AI.
Dreaming Up Radical AI Futures engaged participants in a speculative sci-fi activity adapted from A People's Guide To AI by Mimi Onuoha and Mother Cyborg and created a communal data visualization based on their imagined futures.

Download Dreaming Up Radical AI Futures zine
A Day in the Life with AI explored casual daily encounters with AI. Participants at CtrlZ.AI created handmade postcards representing their daily AI lives. The activity was inspired by the project Dear Data by Giorgia Lupi and Stefanie Posavec.
